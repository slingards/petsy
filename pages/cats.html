Cat Welfare & Ownership Notice

Cats and kittens require daily care, clean food and water, safe housing, litter care, social attention, proper handling, and access to qualified veterinary support. Before requesting any cat, please review its age, temperament, health needs, grooming needs, and long-term care requirements.

Buyers are encouraged to ask questions, request available health or vaccination records, confirm the cat’s current availability, and make sure their home is ready before completing any adoption or inquiry request.
