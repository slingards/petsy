Bird Welfare & Ownership Notice

Birds require proper housing, daily care, clean food and water, safe handling, social interaction, and access to qualified veterinary care. Before requesting any bird, please review its care needs, lifespan, noise level, diet, and space requirements.

Some bird species may be regulated in certain locations. Buyers are responsible for following local ownership, transport, import, export, and animal welfare laws before completing any request.
