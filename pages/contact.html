Before You Contact Us

To help us respond faster, please include clear details about your request. If you are asking about a specific pet listing, include the animal name, category, page link, and any order or inquiry details available.

  • For listing questions, include the animal name and category.
  • For order support, include the name and contact details used during checkout.
  • For payment proof questions, include your transaction reference where available.
  • For animal welfare or safety concerns, include screenshots, page links, or any helpful evidence.

Trust And Safety Reports

If you believe a listing may be misleading, unsafe, illegal, or connected to animal welfare concerns, please report it to CompanionReviewHub immediately. We take trust, safety, and animal welfare concerns seriously.

  • Report suspicious payment requests.
  • Report misleading pet information.
  • Report animal welfare concerns.
  • Report possible illegal wildlife or regulated animal activity.
  • Report impersonation, fake listings, or false documents.

Important Notice

CompanionReviewHub encourages all users to verify listing details, request health records, confirm animal availability, review shipping terms, and understand local laws before completing any pet-related transaction.

CompanionReviewHub does not support illegal wildlife trade, unsafe animal transport, misleading listings, or irresponsible animal ownership.
