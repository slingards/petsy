Reptile Welfare & Ownership Notice

Reptiles require species-specific care, secure enclosures, correct temperature, humidity control, proper lighting, clean water, suitable diet, safe handling, and access to qualified reptile or exotic animal veterinary support. Before requesting any reptile, please review its habitat needs, adult size, feeding requirements, temperament, and long-term care responsibilities.

Some reptiles, turtles, tortoises, snakes, and lizards may be regulated, restricted, or require permits in certain locations. Buyers are responsible for confirming local ownership rules, transport requirements, import or export restrictions, and animal welfare laws before completing any adoption or inquiry request.
