Shipping Policy

Safe, Clear, And Responsible Pet Delivery Guidance

CompanionReviewHub encourages safe animal transport, clear delivery communication, proper documentation, and responsible buyer preparation before any pet is shipped, delivered, or collected.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Purpose Of This Shipping Policy

This Shipping Policy explains how CompanionReviewHub approaches delivery guidance, transport expectations, animal welfare, documentation, buyer responsibilities, and communication around pet delivery.

Because animals are living beings, shipping and delivery must be handled carefully. CompanionReviewHub encourages users to confirm all shipping details, animal health information, delivery timelines, and legal requirements before completing any pet-related transaction.

2. Shipping Is Subject To Confirmation

Shipping availability may depend on the animal type, destination, seller location, buyer location, weather conditions, transport rules, airline or courier availability, veterinary requirements, and local regulations.

Buyers should not assume that every animal can be shipped to every location. Before completing payment or final arrangements, users should confirm:

  • The animal’s current availability.
  • The seller or source location.
  • The buyer’s delivery location.
  • Estimated delivery method and timeline.
  • Any required health certificate, vaccination record, permit, or transport document.

3. Animal Welfare During Transport

CompanionReviewHub does not support unsafe, harmful, overcrowded, or careless animal transport. Animals should be moved only under safe and humane conditions.

  • Animals should travel in suitable carriers, crates, or enclosures.
  • Animals should have proper ventilation and safe handling.
  • Transport should consider heat, cold, travel duration, feeding, hydration, and rest needs.
  • Sick, injured, too-young, or unfit animals should not be shipped without proper veterinary guidance.
  • Long-distance shipping should follow applicable animal welfare and transport rules.

4. Delivery Timelines

Delivery timelines may vary depending on the animal, distance, documentation, payment confirmation, transport availability, weather, airline rules, customs checks, and local regulations.

Any delivery date provided should be treated as an estimate until all required checks and arrangements are confirmed.

  • Local delivery may take less time when the animal and buyer are in the same area.
  • National delivery may require additional coordination and transport checks.
  • International delivery may require documents, inspections, permits, quarantine rules, or import/export clearance.

5. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs may vary depending on the delivery location, animal size, carrier requirements, transport method, veterinary documents, insurance, handling needs, and destination rules.

Buyers should review all shipping costs before completing any order. Shipping fees may include transport handling, carrier preparation, delivery coordination, insurance, or documentation-related costs where applicable.

6. Required Documents

Some deliveries may require documents before an animal can be transported. Required documents may depend on the animal type, location, destination, and transport method.

  • Health certificate or veterinary check record.
  • Vaccination records where applicable.
  • Ownership or transfer document where applicable.
  • Import or export documents for international transport.
  • Permit, license, or legal approval for regulated animals.
  • Airline, courier, or transport approval where required.

Buyers should request and review relevant documents before accepting delivery.

7. Buyer Responsibilities

Buyers are responsible for providing accurate delivery information and preparing properly before receiving any animal.

  • Provide correct full name, phone number, email, and delivery address.
  • Confirm that the animal is legal to own in your location.
  • Be available to receive the animal at the agreed delivery time.
  • Prepare safe housing, food, water, and care supplies before arrival.
  • Review the animal’s condition as soon as it arrives.
  • Keep all receipts, delivery messages, and transaction details.

8. Address Accuracy

Buyers must provide accurate delivery information. CompanionReviewHub is not responsible for delays or failed delivery caused by incorrect names, wrong addresses, missing phone numbers, unreachable contacts, or incomplete delivery details.

If your delivery details change after placing an order or inquiry, contact CompanionReviewHub as soon as possible.

9. Local Pickup Or Direct Collection

In some cases, local pickup or direct collection may be safer or more appropriate than shipping. Buyers should ask whether pickup is available and confirm meeting details in writing.

  • Confirm the pickup location clearly.
  • Ask for updated animal information before pickup.
  • Review health and ownership documents where applicable.
  • Avoid unsafe meeting places or rushed arrangements.

10. International Shipping

International pet shipping may involve strict rules. Buyers should confirm whether the animal can legally enter their country before making any commitment.

International shipping may require:

  • Import permits.
  • Export clearance.
  • Veterinary health certificates.
  • Vaccination or microchip records.
  • Quarantine compliance.
  • Customs or border inspection.

CompanionReviewHub encourages users to contact official authorities or qualified professionals before arranging international animal transport.

11. Exotic Or Regulated Animal Shipping

Exotic, wild, protected, or regulated animals may require special permits, legal approval, trained handling, secure transport, and proper care facilities. Some animals may not be legal to transport or own in certain locations.

CompanionReviewHub does not support illegal wildlife trade or unsafe exotic animal transport. Users must confirm all legal requirements before making any inquiry or delivery arrangement.

  • Verify ownership laws in your location.
  • Confirm transport permission before payment.
  • Request permits or legal documents where required.
  • Ensure proper housing and care arrangements are ready.

12. Delivery Problems Or Delays

Delivery delays may happen because of weather, documentation issues, transport availability, animal welfare checks, customs clearance, payment verification, or incorrect buyer information.

If there is a delivery issue, contact CompanionReviewHub with your order details, contact information, animal name, and any available tracking or delivery communication.

Report Delivery Issue

13. Receiving Your Pet

When receiving an animal, buyers should inspect the animal carefully and make sure the delivery matches the agreed information.

  • Check the animal’s condition immediately.
  • Confirm any documents provided.
  • Provide water, rest, and a calm environment after travel.
  • Contact a veterinarian if the animal appears sick, stressed, or injured.
  • Keep all communication and delivery records.

14. Important Notice

This Shipping Policy is provided for general platform guidance. It does not replace airline rules, courier rules, veterinary advice, government import/export regulations, customs rules, or legal requirements.

Buyers and sellers are responsible for confirming all transport, legal, health, and animal welfare requirements before shipping or receiving any animal.
