Petsy Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

This Privacy Policy explains how Petsy collects, uses, protects, and handles your information when you browse our website, contact us, submit an order request, or use any of our pet marketplace services.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Petsy is a pet marketplace designed to help visitors browse available pets, learn more about animal listings, request purchase or adoption information, and contact our support team. By using our website, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you contact us, complete checkout forms, submit adoption or purchase requests, join our waiting list, or communicate with us through email, WhatsApp, Telegram, or other contact methods.

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number or preferred contact method
  • Shipping or delivery details
  • Pet preference, breed interest, color choice, and age preference
  • Payment or order confirmation details
  • Messages, questions, or support requests you send to us

3. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain basic technical information may be collected automatically through your browser or hosting provider. This may include your device type, browser type, IP address, pages visited, date and time of visit, and general usage activity.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide better service, process inquiries, manage orders, and improve the user experience on Petsy.

  • To respond to your questions and support requests
  • To confirm pet availability, age, color, documents, and delivery options
  • To process cart, checkout, and order requests
  • To contact you after purchase regarding your preferred pet color or listing details
  • To improve our website, listings, services, and customer support
  • To prevent fraud, misuse, unauthorized activity, or unsafe transactions

5. Pet Purchase and Adoption Information

Some pets may require additional documentation, legal checks, transport arrangements, health certificates, or ownership verification depending on the animal type and your location. We may use your provided information to confirm whether a listed animal can legally and safely be delivered to you.

6. Payments and Order Details

Petsy may collect order-related information needed to confirm a purchase request. We do not sell your payment information. Where third-party payment, form, or communication services are used, their own privacy policies may also apply.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to remember cart items, wishlist items, preferences, and improve browsing performance. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but some website features may not work properly.

8. Local Storage

Petsy may use your browser’s local storage to save cart items, wishlist items, and browsing preferences. This information stays on your device unless you clear your browser data.

9. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may only share necessary information with trusted service providers when required to complete a service.

  • Delivery or live-animal transportation providers
  • Veterinary or documentation support partners
  • Payment, form, or hosting service providers
  • Legal authorities when required by law

10. Animal Transport and Legal Compliance

Some animals, especially exotic pets, birds, reptiles, and wild cat species, may be subject to local, national, or international ownership and transport rules. We may request or verify information needed to comply with applicable laws, health requirements, and transportation standards.

11. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no website or online transmission method is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as needed to respond to inquiries, manage orders, provide support, meet legal requirements, prevent fraud, or maintain business records.

13. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to your personal information, correct inaccurate details, request deletion, withdraw consent, or object to certain types of processing.

14. Children’s Privacy

Petsy is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. Pet purchases, adoption requests, and animal ownership inquiries must be handled by adults.

15. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of external websites.

16. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

17. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, your personal information, or how Petsy handles your data, please contact us through the contact section on our website.
