CompanionReviewHub Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters To Us

This Privacy Policy explains how CompanionReviewHub collects, uses, protects, and handles information when you browse our website, contact support, save preferences, submit a pet profile request, or use our responsible pet discovery and request-review features.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

CompanionReviewHub is a responsible pet discovery and request-review website. Our goal is to help visitors review companion animal profiles, learn basic care information, ask questions, and contact support before making any final arrangement.

CompanionReviewHub does not encourage rushed pet decisions, unsafe animal transport, illegal wildlife trade, or irresponsible ownership. By using this website, you agree to the privacy practices described on this page.

2. Information You May Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us, submit a profile request, complete a request-review form, ask for support, or communicate with us through email, WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram, live chat, or other contact methods.

  • Full name or preferred contact name
  • Email address
  • Phone number or preferred contact method
  • Country, state, city, or general location details needed for legal, delivery, or support review
  • Companion animal preference, breed interest, age preference, size preference, color preference, or listing reference
  • Messages, questions, support requests, or care-related concerns you send to us
  • Information needed to review whether a request may be suitable, safe, and legally permitted

3. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain basic technical information may be collected automatically through your browser, hosting provider, analytics tools, security tools, or similar technologies.

  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • General usage activity, such as pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Approximate location signals such as country or region when available
  • IP address, referral source, date, and time of visit
  • Website performance, error, and security-related information

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, review requests, improve website safety, support responsible ownership, and provide a better user experience.

  • To respond to your questions, messages, and support requests
  • To review companion animal profile requests before any final arrangement
  • To confirm general availability, care requirements, documentation needs, and support options
  • To understand whether a request may require legal, welfare, transport, or location-based review
  • To improve our website, content, profile information, contact process, and support experience
  • To help prevent fraud, spam, misuse, unauthorized activity, unsafe requests, or illegal activity
  • To maintain business records, security logs, and compliance records where necessary

5. Pet Profile Requests And Review Information

Some companion animal requests may require additional review before any final arrangement can be discussed. This may include care preparation, location restrictions, delivery suitability, veterinary documents, health records, ownership eligibility, or other animal welfare considerations.

For regulated or exotic animal-related inquiries, additional legal and welfare review may be required. CompanionReviewHub does not support illegal wildlife trade, unsafe ownership, or the transfer of animals where prohibited by law.

6. Payment And Request-Review Details

CompanionReviewHub does not ask users to send payment through the public website at the initial request stage. Any payment-related discussion should only happen after support review, availability confirmation, legal checks where needed, and clear communication with the user.

We do not sell payment information. If third-party payment, form, hosting, analytics, or communication services are used, those services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

7. Cookies, Analytics, And Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies, analytics tools, local storage, or similar technologies to improve website performance, understand general site usage, remember preferences, support dark mode, save request-list or wishlist items, and improve browsing experience.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies or local storage are disabled.

8. Local Storage

CompanionReviewHub may use your browser’s local storage to save request-list items, wishlist items, dark mode preference, and other browsing preferences. Local storage usually stays on your device unless you clear your browser data.

Local storage is mainly used to make the website easier to use. It does not mean a request has been approved, confirmed, reserved, or completed.

9. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when it is necessary to provide support, operate the website, review requests, comply with law, or protect users, animals, and our services.

  • Hosting, analytics, form, security, or website service providers
  • Communication tools used to respond to your inquiry
  • Veterinary, documentation, welfare, or transport-related support providers when needed for a specific request
  • Legal, regulatory, or safety authorities when required by law or necessary to prevent harm, fraud, or illegal activity

10. Animal Welfare, Transport, And Legal Compliance

Some animal requests may be subject to local, national, or international ownership, transport, welfare, health, and documentation rules. We may request or review information needed to understand whether a request can be handled responsibly and legally.

CompanionReviewHub may decline, pause, or refuse requests that appear unsafe, prohibited, incomplete, suspicious, or inconsistent with animal welfare and legal requirements.

11. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, alteration, or disclosure. However, no website, email system, messaging app, or online transmission method is completely secure.

Please avoid sending highly sensitive information unless it is necessary and requested for a legitimate support or compliance purpose.

12. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably needed to respond to inquiries, review requests, provide support, improve safety, meet legal obligations, prevent fraud, resolve disputes, or maintain necessary business records.

When information is no longer needed, we may delete, anonymize, or securely store it according to our operational and legal needs.

13. Your Privacy Choices And Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access to your personal information, correct inaccurate information, request deletion, withdraw consent, object to certain processing, or ask questions about how your information is used.

To make a privacy-related request, contact us using the contact page. We may need to verify your request before taking action.

14. Children’s Privacy

CompanionReviewHub is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. Pet profile requests, adoption-related inquiries, ownership questions, and animal care arrangements should be handled by adults.

If you believe a minor has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

15. Third-Party Links And Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, services, references, tools, or support platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, security, or policies of external websites.

Please review the privacy policy of any third-party service you choose to use.

16. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect website changes, operational updates, safety improvements, legal requirements, or better privacy explanations.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

17. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, your personal information, or how CompanionReviewHub handles data, please contact us through our contact page.
