Legal Compliance

Responsible Pet Ownership Must Follow The Law

CompanionReviewHub encourages all users to follow applicable animal ownership laws, transport rules, welfare standards, permit requirements, and local regulations before making any pet-related decision.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Purpose Of This Page

This Legal Compliance page explains CompanionReviewHub’s position on responsible pet discovery, animal ownership laws, regulated animals, transport requirements, documentation, and user responsibility.

CompanionReviewHub is committed to encouraging lawful, transparent, and responsible animal-related activity. Users should never assume that an animal can be purchased, transported, imported, exported, or owned without checking the rules that apply in their location.

2. User Responsibility

Every buyer, seller, visitor, or user of CompanionReviewHub is responsible for understanding and following the laws that apply to them. Laws may differ by country, state, city, region, animal type, breed, age, species, and transport method.

  • Buyers must confirm whether the animal is legal to own in their location.
  • Sellers must confirm that they are legally allowed to offer, transfer, or deliver the animal.
  • Users must check whether permits, licenses, vaccination records, health certificates, or ownership documents are required.
  • Users must follow transport, import, export, quarantine, and animal welfare regulations where applicable.

3. No Illegal Wildlife Trade

CompanionReviewHub does not support illegal wildlife trade, protected species trafficking, unsafe exotic animal sales, or any transaction that violates wildlife protection laws.

Some animals may be protected, restricted, endangered, regulated, or prohibited by law. This may include certain wild cats, reptiles, birds, exotic mammals, and other species.

  • Users must not use CompanionReviewHub to request, promote, or complete illegal wildlife transactions.
  • Users must not misrepresent regulated animals as ordinary household pets.
  • Users must not avoid permits, legal checks, welfare rules, or transport requirements.
  • Any exotic or regulated animal inquiry should be handled with serious legal verification.

4. Regulated And Exotic Animals

Exotic or regulated animals may require special approval, permits, trained handling, secure housing, veterinary documentation, and official transport clearance. In some places, private ownership may be illegal.

Before requesting any exotic or regulated animal, users should confirm the following:

  • Whether the animal is legal to own in their country, state, city, or region.
  • Whether a permit, license, or registration is required.
  • Whether special enclosure, feeding, veterinary, or care standards apply.
  • Whether import, export, quarantine, or inspection rules apply.
  • Whether the animal is protected under wildlife or conservation laws.

5. Domestic Pets And Breed Restrictions

Even common pets may be subject to local laws. Certain dog breeds, cat breeds, reptiles, birds, or other animals may be restricted in some apartments, cities, states, or countries.

Buyers should confirm that they are allowed to own the pet in their home, apartment, community, city, or country before making any commitment.

  • Some locations have breed-specific rules.
  • Some housing providers restrict animal size, breed, or number of pets.
  • Some animals may require vaccination, microchipping, registration, or licensing.
  • Some regions require quarantine or veterinary documents before entry.

6. Documentation Requirements

CompanionReviewHub encourages users to request and verify proper documents before completing any pet-related transaction. Documentation may vary depending on the animal type, seller, location, and transport method.

  • Health certificate or veterinary check record.
  • Vaccination record where applicable.
  • Ownership or transfer document where applicable.
  • Import or export documents where required.
  • Transport approval or airline documents where applicable.
  • Permit, license, or registration documents for regulated animals.

If required documents are missing, unclear, or suspicious, users should pause the transaction and request clarification.

7. Shipping, Delivery, And Transport Compliance

Animal transport must follow applicable laws and welfare standards. Pets should not be transported in unsafe, overcrowded, poorly ventilated, or harmful conditions.

Depending on the animal and destination, transport may require veterinary clearance, travel carriers, airline approval, import documents, quarantine rules, inspection, or special handling.

  • Users should confirm delivery terms before making payment arrangements.
  • Users should confirm whether the animal can legally enter the destination location.
  • Users should request shipping or delivery information in writing.
  • Users should never agree to unsafe transport conditions.

8. Prohibited Use

CompanionReviewHub should not be used for any activity that violates animal welfare laws, wildlife laws, consumer protection rules, payment rules, or public safety requirements.

  • Illegal wildlife trade or protected species trafficking.
  • False or misleading animal listings.
  • Fake health records, fake permits, or fake ownership documents.
  • Unsafe animal shipping or harmful animal handling.
  • Pressure-based payment demands without proper verification.
  • Transactions involving animals that the buyer is not legally allowed to own.
  • Any activity connected to animal cruelty, neglect, or abuse.

9. Buyer Verification Checklist

Before completing any pet-related transaction, buyers should take time to verify important details.

  • Confirm the animal’s current availability.
  • Request updated photos or videos where possible.
  • Ask for health, vaccination, and care information.
  • Confirm legal ownership or transfer documents where applicable.
  • Confirm that the animal is legal to own in your location.
  • Review shipping, delivery, refund, and support terms.
  • Keep records of communication, receipts, and transaction details.

10. Platform Limitation

CompanionReviewHub provides a digital platform for pet discovery, information, listings, inquiries, and order support. CompanionReviewHub does not replace official government agencies, licensed veterinarians, animal welfare authorities, legal professionals, or customs officials.

Users should contact the relevant authorities or qualified professionals when they need legal, veterinary, customs, import, export, or animal welfare advice.

11. Reporting Legal Concerns

If you believe a listing or transaction may involve illegal wildlife trade, false documents, unsafe transport, misleading pet information, or animal welfare concerns, please contact CompanionReviewHub immediately.

Include helpful information such as the listing name, page link, screenshots, messages, contact details, payment references, or any documents shared with you.
