Trust & Safety

Safer Pet Discovery Starts With Transparency

CompanionReviewHub is built to support responsible pet discovery, clear communication, buyer awareness, animal welfare, and legal compliance. This page explains how users can browse, verify, report, and make informed decisions safely.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Our Trust & Safety Promise

CompanionReviewHub is committed to creating a safer and more transparent pet discovery experience. We encourage every user to verify animal details, request documents, review policies, confirm legal requirements, and avoid rushed decisions.

CompanionReviewHub does not support misleading listings, unsafe animal transport, illegal wildlife trade, animal cruelty, fake documents, pressure-based payments, or any activity that places buyers, sellers, or animals at risk.

2. How CompanionReviewHub Supports Safer Discovery

CompanionReviewHub provides structured pages, listing details, policy information, and support channels to help users make better decisions before committing to any pet-related transaction.

  • Clear pet listing pages with available animal details.
  • Learn More pages with care, feeding, health, and document reminders.
  • Trust, safety, legal, welfare, refund, and shipping policy pages.
  • Contact support for listing questions, order concerns, or safety reports.
  • Responsible reminders before checkout, shipping, and animal delivery.

3. Buyer Safety Checklist

Before submitting payment or final order details, buyers should verify important information. A responsible pet decision should be calm, documented, and clear.

  • Confirm the animal’s current availability.
  • Request updated photos or videos where possible.
  • Ask for health records, vaccination details, and care history where applicable.
  • Confirm the animal’s age, breed, location, temperament, and special care needs.
  • Review shipping, delivery, refund, and cancellation terms.
  • Confirm that the animal is legal to own in your location.
  • Keep all receipts, messages, payment references, and documents.

4. Seller And Listing Standards

Listings should be honest, clear, and not misleading. Any pet source or seller involved in a listing should provide accurate information and avoid hiding important details.

  • Animal age, breed, health, location, and availability should be represented truthfully.
  • Photos should represent the animal clearly and should not mislead buyers.
  • Known health conditions or special care needs should be disclosed.
  • Sellers should not pressure buyers into rushed payment decisions.
  • Sellers should comply with animal welfare and ownership laws.

5. Payment Safety

Buyers should always review payment instructions carefully and keep proof of any transaction. Payment should not be rushed if important information is missing or unclear.

  • Confirm animal availability before sending payment.
  • Review the refund policy before completing payment.
  • Keep receipts, transaction IDs, screenshots, and communication records.
  • Do not proceed if payment details look suspicious or inconsistent.
  • Contact CompanionReviewHub support if you are unsure about any payment instruction.

6. Communication Safety

Good communication protects both buyers and listed pet sources. Users should ask clear questions and avoid decisions based only on one image, one message, or pressure from another person.

  • Ask for written confirmation of important details.
  • Avoid pressure tactics such as “pay immediately or lose the pet.”
  • Be careful with anyone refusing to answer basic questions.
  • Save messages, receipts, images, and order details.
  • Report suspicious or aggressive communication to CompanionReviewHub.

7. Animal Welfare Protection

CompanionReviewHub believes every animal should be treated humanely. Animals should have proper food, clean water, safe housing, veterinary attention, and safe handling before, during, and after any transaction.

  • Animals should not be transported in unsafe or harmful conditions.
  • Sick or injured animals should receive proper veterinary care.
  • Buyers should understand the animal’s care needs before making a commitment.
  • CompanionReviewHub does not support animal cruelty, neglect, or irresponsible ownership.
View Animal Welfare Policy

8. Legal And Regulated Animal Safety

Some animals may be regulated, restricted, protected, or illegal to own in certain locations. This can include exotic animals, wild animals, certain reptiles, certain birds, and animals requiring permits or special care.

CompanionReviewHub does not support illegal wildlife trade. Users are responsible for confirming all laws before requesting, purchasing, transporting, importing, exporting, or owning any animal.

  • Confirm local ownership laws before submitting an order.
  • Check whether permits or licenses are required.
  • Confirm import, export, quarantine, or transport rules where applicable.
  • Avoid any transaction involving protected species or illegal wildlife trade.
View Legal Compliance

9. Shipping And Delivery Safety

Pet delivery should be handled with care. Shipping may depend on location, animal type, weather, documentation, transport rules, and animal welfare requirements.

  • Confirm delivery timelines before completing an order.
  • Ask what transport method will be used.
  • Make sure the animal will travel in safe conditions.
  • Confirm whether health certificates, permits, or transport documents are required.
  • Prepare food, water, housing, and veterinary support before the animal arrives.

10. Warning Signs To Watch For

Users should be careful when they notice signs that may suggest unsafe, misleading, or suspicious activity.

  • Refusal to provide animal details, documents, or updated information.
  • Pressure to pay immediately without verification.
  • Prices that seem unrealistic or inconsistent with the animal.
  • Conflicting names, locations, images, or contact details.
  • Requests to avoid normal communication or documentation steps.
  • Claims that permits, health records, or transport rules are not important.
  • Any offer involving illegal wildlife or protected species.

11. Prohibited Activity

CompanionReviewHub should not be used for activities that harm animals, mislead users, break laws, or damage platform trust.

  • Illegal wildlife trade or protected species trafficking.
  • Fake listings, fake documents, or false ownership claims.
  • Animal cruelty, neglect, unsafe transport, or harmful handling.
  • Misleading payment requests or false payment proof.
  • Harassment, threats, spam, or abusive communication.
  • Any activity that violates applicable animal welfare laws or ownership rules.

12. Reporting A Concern

If you believe a listing, message, payment request, delivery arrangement, or animal situation may be unsafe, misleading, illegal, or harmful, please contact CompanionReviewHub immediately.

To help us review your concern, include as many details as possible.

  • Listing name or animal category.
  • Page link or screenshot.
  • Messages or communication records.
  • Payment reference or receipt if relevant.
  • Any health, legal, delivery, or welfare concern.

13. What CompanionReviewHub May Do After A Report

When a concern is reported, CompanionReviewHub may review the information provided and take reasonable steps depending on the issue.

  • Request more information from the reporting user.
  • Review listing details or communication records.
  • Pause or remove content that appears misleading, unsafe, or inappropriate.
  • Decline support for activity that appears unlawful or harmful.
  • Encourage users to contact official authorities where necessary.

14. Important Safety Reminder

CompanionReviewHub provides a platform for pet discovery, information, inquiry, and guidance. Users should not treat any website listing as a substitute for personal verification, veterinary advice, legal advice, or official government rules.

Before committing to any pet-related transaction, confirm the animal’s details, review documents, understand costs, verify legal requirements, and prepare responsibly for ownership.
