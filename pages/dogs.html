Dog Welfare & Ownership Notice

Dogs and puppies require daily care, proper feeding, clean water, safe housing, exercise, training, social attention, and access to qualified veterinary support. Before requesting any dog, please review its age, breed traits, temperament, activity level, grooming needs, and long-term care requirements.

Buyers are encouraged to ask questions, request available health or vaccination records, confirm the dog’s current availability, and make sure their home, schedule, and budget are ready before completing any adoption or inquiry request.
